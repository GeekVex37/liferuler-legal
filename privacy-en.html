LifeRuler Privacy Policy

Established: June 2026
Last updated: June 2026

LifeRuler ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the App handles and how it is used.

1. Information You Enter

You can enter dreams, goals, ToDos, events, categories, notes, dates, and times into the App. This information is stored on your device only.

The developer does not view, collect, or transmit your personal content — unless you choose to contact support directly.

2. Information Stored Locally

The following settings are saved on your device:

  • Language preference
  • App settings (theme, display options, etc.)
  • Reminder settings
  • Profile settings (birth date, life expectancy, motto)

None of this information is sent to external servers.

3. Future Features

In the future, the App may add features such as analytics, crash reporting, in-app purchases, or ads. If any such feature is introduced, this policy will be updated accordingly.

If in-app purchases are introduced, payments will be processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer will not receive your credit card or payment details.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services to help with app improvement, analytics, or payment processing. These services include, but are not limited to:

  • Apple App Store / In-App Purchase
  • Expo / EAS
  • PostHog
  • RevenueCat

Each service handles data according to its own privacy policy.

5. Contacting Support

If you send feedback, a bug report, or a support request, only the content you write is shared (via email). The developer uses this information only to respond to your message and improve the App.

6. Data Sharing

Your personal information is not sold or shared with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary for operating the App (e.g., third-party services listed above).

7. Data Management

You can edit or delete your data at any time within the App. Deleting the App may remove locally stored data, depending on your device and OS settings.

8. Children

This App is not specifically designed for children. If a minor uses the App, parental consent is recommended.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you in the App or on this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
